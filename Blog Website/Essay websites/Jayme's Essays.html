    Becoming a CIITeen: What Track or Strand to Pursue

    Comparison & Contrast Essay

    Written by Jayme Lenon | May 2,2021 - 3:06 PM

    In most schools, the tracks they offer are usually the same: STEM, ABM, HUMSS, and GAS. CIIT, however, offers the tracks Media and Visual Arts, Animation, and Programming. Students that are aiming for the arts and technology industry will need to pick a strand that is appropriate to what they aspire. Media and Visual Arts, Animation, and Programming strands are different and similar in terms of what subjects they offer.

    Media and Visual Arts tackle with arts and design and has what most other art schools offer. Students will study the specialized subjects: Introduction to Multimedia Arts, Art History, Drawing, Elements and Principles of Graphic Design, Digital Photography, Writing for New Media, Color Theory, Typography and Layout, and Fundamentals of Film and Video Production. This strand will lead students to become photographers, graphic designers, and art directors.

    Animation has students tackle a well grounded foundation in designing characters, scenes, props and settings. The specialized subjects include History of Graphic Design, Digital Animation, Introduction to Multimedia Arts, Digital Storyboarding, 2D Animation, Color Theory, 3D Modelling, and Typography and Layout. Animators, storyboard compositor, and animation directer are one of the few careers this strand will lead.

    Programming deals with code, the creation of new programs and apps and software development. This strand’s subject include Introduction to Computing, Computer Programming 1 & 2, Application, Development and Emerging Technologies, Web Design and Scripting, Architecture and Organization of Machine Language, Web Programming, Introduction to Game Design, and Information Management. You can become a system programmer, software engineer or a game developer if you pursue this strand.

    With how different these strands are, it is important to know about them so that we can decide carefully on what career we want. All of these strands teach general academic subjects and they are all taught in a lab-based format. These strands differ in subjects: subjects that partain in what career you want.

    What Type of Student are you?

    Classification Essay

    Written by Jayme Lenon | May 1,2021 - 7:40 PM

    A student is usually the one that makes up the school, they come to school to learn, meet other students, become friends with them and discover new interests. There are many types of students that can be described by their behaviour, interests, and overall participation in class. These types of students can still be found even in an online environment during this stressful situation.

    There is always that type of student that cracks jokes every so often or disrupts the class; the funny student. Funny students are very talkative to others and often have a lot of friends. They can sometimes be bothersome during the class, making noise or disturbing other students.

    In the same way, there is the enthusiastic student. An enthusiastic student is the one with the straight A’s, perfect attendance, and is very extroverted. They are often picked to be the leader of the class’ section and are very motivated to do their tasks successfully. They are also very friendly to their teachers and often ask questions to them.

    On the other hand, there are students that do the opposite; the procrastinator student. These students delay their tasks and sometimes participate in class. Their internet is always seemingly slow and unresponsive, which may lead them to actually having to delay what they need to do in order to try and get their internet to work.

    Similarly, there’s the insecure student, a more drastic version of the procrastinator student. These students are very shy, they rarely open their cameras and barely talk in their microphone. They are not confident and are very anxious towards other students, and they also overthink if they are under pressure from school works.

    These types of students are still present even in the current situation which led to classes being conducted online. There will be students that are very active like the funny and enthusiastic ones, and there will be students that are having a hard time getting used to the online format. Students are all unique from one another, even if times are hard.
